Silver Alpha is a swing trade strategy built on top of Aerondight's multi-factor scoring engine. It uses conviction scores and signal transitions to systematically enter and exit positions in US equities.

## How It Works

The strategy monitors the entire ~900 stock universe for signal transitions. When a stock's score crosses the entry threshold and both the swing trade and long-term models agree, a position is opened. Exits are triggered by score deterioration, time-based stops, or regime shifts.

All decisions are fully systematic — no discretionary overrides.

## Performance (2023-2025 Out-of-Sample)

The strategy was run as a paper simulation from January 2023 through 2025, producing 135 completed trades:

- **59.3% win rate** across all trades
- **15.25% average return** per trade
- Exits driven by score deterioration, not arbitrary stop-losses

![Silver Alpha equity curve vs SPY](/backtest/silver_alpha_equity_curve.png)

## Full Transparency

Every trade is logged with entry date, exit date, entry price, exit price, return, SPY return over the same period, excess return, days held, and exit reason.

The complete trade log is available for download: [silver_alpha_trades_2023_2025.csv](/backtest/silver_alpha_trades_2023_2025.csv)
